Modern Art as a Woman



Blotches of paint show hues so bold,
She sketches her story, and her tale unfolds,
One canvas is blank, but her mind is untamed,
She paints what she's feeling, it strikes away her pain.


Colors and shapes portray a restless dance,
Emotions on display, every time you glance,
Challenge the norms, break old regimes,
Her hand is tensed, sweaty like steam.


Concepts, abstract, her ideas are free,
Her culture's important, she assures diversity is seen,
The imagery created, we in our dreams,
Each stroke has a story to tell, or so it seems.


She can't resist, her boundaries are pushed,
Her work is unknown, she keeps it unlooked,
If someone were to see, it would start a debate,
So, she takes her time, she'll make it great.


The painting is finished, her paintbrush is curled,
She creates a timeless masterpiece, within our ever-changing world.
